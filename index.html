Welcome to Weather by Day

Have you ever wondered what the weather was like on the day you were born? When it rained on the last day of your vacation, was it sunny and pleasant at home? How bad the weather must have been to rain out the fifth game of the World Series in 2008?

Satisfy your curiosity with Weather by Day. Just pick a day all the way back to 1950 and a place anywhere in the world.
